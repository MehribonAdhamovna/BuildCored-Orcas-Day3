# BuildCored-Orcas-Day3
VolumeKnuckle — BUILDCORED ORCAS Day 03

What it does. I built a program that turns your hand into a remote control for your music. When you make a fist, you can slide it up and down to change the volume, and as soon as you open your hand, the music pauses automatically.

Hardware concept. The main idea here is mapping. I’m taking a physical "analog" movement (my hand height) and converting it into a digital number that the computer understands as a volume percentage.

Screenshot. https://drive.google.com/file/d/1eQIgPnJeYWnOaS0pTpyRxhW_-4me83Zy/view?usp=sharing; https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Q_iXslWzNB7sMuv27vifZoV_YfqKSaIU/view?usp=sharing;

What I would do differently. If I did this again, I’d add more gestures, like waving left or right to skip songs. I’d also make the music fade out slowly when pausing instead of just cutting off instantly so it sounds a bit smoother.
